About this project

This is an experiment. For thirty-odd weeks, I’ll publish chapters from this children's novel, review and rewrite the texts, and reflect aloud on the processes and vagaries of writing and editing.

What can you do here? Check the project announcement How (not) to write a children's book to figure out what’s going on here, subscribe to update notifications, and submit suggestions and edits through either Github or email.

If you've only just arrived, the best thing might be to skip all this and simply dive into the story—probably starting at the beginning, Chapter One.
